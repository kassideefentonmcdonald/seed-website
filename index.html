Our Story

Fresno
Produces
Champions.

Fresno has produced Super Bowl champions, All-Pros, and first-round picks. But they made it without the support system today's athletes need.

The game has changed. College recruiting starts earlier. Training is year-round. Exposure requires access to camps, showcases, and platforms most families simply cannot afford.

The next generation has the same talent — but now the game requires more. S.E.E.D. exists to provide the access, structure, and support that was never there before.

SEED removesfinancial barriers.

What We Fund

Five Pillars
of Development

⚡

Training & Performance

Year-round speed, strength, and position-specific coaching. After-school programs and camps built to develop athletes at every level.

👟

Equipment & Gear

Uniforms, training gear, and essential equipment. Every athlete deserves to show up ready — not held back by what they can't afford.

🥗

Nutrition & Recovery

Post-training nutrition and performance support. Fueling athletes properly shouldn't depend on family income.

🎥

Exposure & Recruiting

Film production, showcases, and recruiting platform access so college programs can find Valley talent — and Valley talent can be found.

🚌

Transportation & Mobility

Getting athletes to college campuses, camps, and recruiting events. Goal: 15-passenger vans so no athlete misses an opportunity because of distance.
